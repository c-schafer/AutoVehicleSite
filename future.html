The Future of Autonomous Cars

The future of Autonomous Vehicles is somewhat uncertain and can be relatively unpredictable. However, we can do our best to see what the process of self-driving vehicles might look like years from now. Even the "experts" in the 1970's believed that we would have flying cars in the 2010's and look where we are at now, so there is no telling for sure what the future holds.

Pro's of a Self-Driving Vehicle Future

Many different advantages of autonomous vehicles include:

  1. Convenience
  2. Efficiency
  3. Traffic Congestion
  4. Driving Capabilities for People with Disabilities

These have been described as the main attraction for autonomous vehicles along with the on-going debate of if having more autonomous cars will lead to lower driving accident rates.
